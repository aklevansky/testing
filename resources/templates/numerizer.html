NUMERIZER

We all have problems, however experienced and fluent we may be, with numbers when they are coming fast in a speech in a foreing language. The application was designed for (future) interpreters and language learners by a professional interpreter with the aim to provide an unlimited source of challenging exercises:
  • Noting down every number in a sequence
  • Shadowing (repeating after the speaker first being just one number behind the original and then progressively trying to stay further behind)
  • Simultaneous or consecutive interpreting
Should you have any questions, suggestions or further ideas, feel free to contact me (see contact section)
The application is free to use by private individuals as well as educational institutions.
